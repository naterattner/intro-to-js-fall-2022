NYC JAM

Pickup basketball is a New York City institution.

The city has a deep culture and history surrounding the game, and spending time at its various parks and playgrounds is a great way to meet people. Its courts aren’t NBA-quality – most have cracks, slopes, crooked rims or other quirks – but learning how to deal with those imperfections is part of the fun.

The map below shows a handful of favorite courts in Brooklyn and Manhattan that are likely to offer New Yorkers a good game.
